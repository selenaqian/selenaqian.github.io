How to get over it:

Notable Symptoms and Recommendations

  • Coughing
    Drink lots of water. You can also add honey to soothe your throat.
  • Sneezing
    Keep warm. Layers are your friend even though it's still hot outside. Try not to sneeze on other people.
  • Tiredness
    Get more sleep. Talk to your 8:30 am professor and explain your situation. Don't go out until you feel better.
